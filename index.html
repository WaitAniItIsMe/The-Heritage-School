Basic Information

The Heritage School, established in 2001, is a unique endeavour of the Kalyan Bharti Trust to recreate the ancient Gurukul tradition of India. Nestled in the lap of nature, the school provides an ideal atmosphere for learners to acquire and imbibe skills necessary for their physical, mental, social and intellectual development

We believe that the world could be made a better place through education which focuses on concepts, ideas and issues that transcend disciplinary, cultural, national and geographical boundaries. To this effect, the school offers a variety of curricula ranging from the national ICSE and ISC to the international IGCSE and IBDP, with an aim to provide comprehensive education opportunities necessary for preparing conscientious, responsible and dynamic future global citizens.

Sports Facilities


At The Heritage, sports and games curriculum is an integral and compulsory part of the education process. Sports help to build character and teach the importance of discipline in life. It inculcates a respect for rules and allows the participants to learn the value to self control.
